Marketing for a Greener World

 

World Environment Day was all about the progress we can make when everyone plays their part NOW for climate. At Sparx, we’ve been stepping into our role as environmental stewards with our marketing shoes on. 

 

We know storytelling and communications are a pivotal driver for circular economy outcomes, and we’re not afraid to prove it. We’ve been watering the seeds of impact with our expertise, and we’re here to share what we’ve grown with you.

 

Keep scrolling for an exclusive Circular Economy Magazine preview, an exciting company update, circular economy resources, purpose-driven events, and some highlights we couldn’t keep to ourselves.

Circular Economy Magazine

Issue 2 Exclusive Preview

 

The second edition of Circular Economy Magazine is coming soon! Explore the latest developments in the world of circular economy implementation, from the standards, certifications, and frameworks guiding circular action to innovations setting a new benchmark for circularity. 


Featuring the inspiring stories of eight organizations and programs providing the guidance we need to take unified circular action and demonstrating what circularity looks like in practice. Plus, enjoy two special content pieces about why standards are so important and gain insights into scaling circularity.

This April, we were proud to join the Recycling Council of Alberta (RCA) alongside 350+ municipalities, businesses, individuals, and other circular economy champions building a waste-free world. 



A leading sustainability organization for over 30 years, RCA works to promote, facilitate, and advocate for a circular economy in Alberta, including hosting its premier Circular Economy Conference each fall.
