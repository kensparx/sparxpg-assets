Circular Economy Magazine Issue 2 Now Live

At last, the second issue of our publication, Circular Economy Magazine, has arrived!

 

Themed around circular economy standards, this edition explores the latest developments in the world of circular economy implementation, including the guidance we need for taking unified action and innovations setting a new benchmark for circularity.

 

Dive into the inspiring stories of ChopValue, Circular Innovation Council, City of Toronto, Cradle to Cradle Products Innovation Institute, GRT (Generating Resources For Tomorrow), KORE Outdoors, Martin Charter on ISO 59000, and Ten Lives Festivals.

 

Plus, enjoy two special content pieces from Michael Leering of CSA Group and Danielle Holly of the Ellen MacArthur Foundation.

 

A special thank you to our sponsors CSA Group, Circular Materials, and the Recycling Council of Alberta for their amazing support of this edition!
