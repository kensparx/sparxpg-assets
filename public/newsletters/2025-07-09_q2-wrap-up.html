Driving Purpose Forward in Q2

 

This past quarter reminded us how powerful collaboration and purpose can be. We hosted the third edition of our free Purpose Connections session, attended and sponsored the Canadian Circular Economy Summit in Montréal, celebrated the launch of Circular Economy Magazine with our partners and contributors, and forged meaningful new relationships along the way. 

 

Keep scrolling to access our latest Purpose Connections session, event highlights, and more!

Now on Demand: Watch the Latest Purpose Connections Session

In this session, we explored the theme “Purpose Pragmatism” with experts Mike Rowlands, Co-Founder of the Canadian Purpose Economy Project and Partner & CEO of Junxion Strategy, Toby Barazzuol, CEO of Eclipse Awards, and Vanessa Marshall, CEO of Jack59 and 2023 BCorp Indigenous Entrepreneur of the Year. 

 

A big thank you to our expert panel and Tessa Vanderkop, Purpose Advisor, Trainer, and Community Builder, for organizing this session with us in collaboration with the Canadian Purpose Economy Project.

 

Be sure to follow Sparx for updates on our next session!

Key Events We Attended in Q2




SIPC/CPEP - Vancouver Spring Event


Date: April 2


Location: Vancouver, British Columbia


Description: Presented by Social Impact Professionals Canada and the Canadian Purpose Economy Project, this event was both educational and inspiring. Our Head of Growth, Alex Nikitina, attended alongside our Founder & President, Hamish Khamisa, who spoke on the topic “Finding Purpose in Turbulent Times: Social Purpose Business as a Beacon".


 


Canadian Circular Economy Summit


Date: April 15 – 17


Location: Montréal, Québec


Description: Sparx was proud to return as a sponsor of this premier event, hosted by Circular Economy Leadership Canada and Circular Innovation Council, where industry leaders gathered to accelerate Canada’s transition to a circular economy. Hamish, Alex, and Meghan O’Neil, our Copywriter, represented Sparx at our branded booth and helped launch the inaugural edition of Circular Economy Magazine. 


 


Business for Social Good Summit


Date: May 16


Location: Vancouver, British Columbia


Description: Hamish and Alex attended this summit, hosted by The Peter P. Dhillon Centre for Business Ethics, which explored differing perspectives on AI and the opportunities and challenges it presents for business and society.


 


Summit on Responsible Investment (SoRI)


Date: June 5


Location: Kelowna, British Columbia


Description: Hosted by the Canadian Securities Exchange, this event brought together industry leaders, investors, and innovators to explore the future of sustainable finance and how capital markets drive meaningful change under the theme “Creating Value, Stewarding Change". Hamish and Brandon Ashcraft, our Marketing Coordinator, attended this premier annual gathering and proudly represented Sparx.





    
    


  

  



      


    
    



    

  

    
      

      
    		
  



  
A Key Event We’re Attending in Q3




Victoria Forum


Date: August 24 – 26


Location: Victoria, British Columbia


Description: Co-hosted by the University of Victoria and the Senate of Canada, this event will take place under the theme “Towards a Better Future: Shifting the Trajectory.” Hamish and Alex are excited to attend and explore how we can accelerate meaningful progress in transforming our economy, society, and way of life.
