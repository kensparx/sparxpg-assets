Building on Momentum This Fall

 

This fall, we continued driving our mission forward together, hosting another Purpose Connections session, participating in Circular Economy Month, and celebrating the growing reach of Circular Economy Magazine. From thought-provoking discussions on AI’s role in purpose-driven business to meaningful conversations at the 2025 Victoria Forum, this truly was a season of learning, collaboration, and impact.

 

Keep scrolling to access our latest Purpose Connections session, read event highlights, and more!

Purpose Connections: Purpose and AI - Transforming the Economy

In this session, we explored how AI can accelerate the transition to a purpose-driven economy, highlighting both the opportunities and challenges for leaders and organizations.

 

We welcomed experts, Carlota de Paula Coelho, Senior Policy Manager at B Lab and EFRAG TEG Member, and Dr. Ron Dembo, Founder and CEO of RiskThinking.ai, who shared valuable insights on this timely theme.

A big thank-you to our expert panel and Tessa Vanderkop, Purpose Advisor, Trainer, and Community Builder, for organizing this session with us.


Don’t miss out! Our next session featuring Dr. Victoria Hurth is coming soon. Register for free here.

Upcoming Events




CPEP Presents the Greater Purpose Launch Party


Date: November 19 


Location: Vancouver, British Columbia


Description: As part of the fifth national Purpose in Business Week, the Canadian Purpose Economy Project (CPEP) and Greater Purpose are hosting a fall event focused on accelerating the transition to the purpose economy. We look forward to hearing from inspiring speakers, enjoying good food, and connecting with the purpose-driven community.


 


Purpose Connections: Beyond Profit - Cutting Through the Chaos to Build a Sustainable World


Date: November 20 


Location: Online


Description: This Purpose in Business Week, join us for a dynamic conversation designed to help leaders cut through the chaos and take decisive action toward building a truly sustainable and regenerative world. Gain insights from Dr. Victoria Hurth, a leading pracademic advancing long-term wellbeing for all, during this free, one-hour online gathering organized by Sparx and Tessa Vanderkop, purpose advisor, trainer, and community builder.


 


Zero Waste Conference


Date: November 27 


Location: Vancouver, British Columbia


Description: This event will bring together innovators, policymakers, and purpose-driven leaders to explore how waste prevention can power Canada’s economic future. The Sparx team is looking forward to inspiring keynotes, hands-on workshops, and forward-thinking discussions on building an economy that works for both people and the planet. Key Events We Attended



  
    

      
        
      
    

  


  


Reeve & Fairware Shoreline Cleanup 


Date: July 17


Location: Vancouver, British Columbia


Description: Alexandra Nikitina, our Head of Growth, and Libby Shabada, our Lead Copywriter, were part of the winning team at the annual Reeve Consulting & Fairware Shoreline Cleanup in support of Ocean Wise, collecting more than 200 cigarette butts, 30 food wrappers, 70 paper items, 60 plastic materials, and more! 


 


PNW Climate Week 
Date: July 16–25


Location: Pacific Northwest


Description: PNW Climate Week hosted dynamic events across more than 10 cities in the Pacific Northwest, bringing together innovators, advocates, and climate-curious citizens, and we were thrilled to help spotlight the incredible events happening in Vancouver.


 


Victoria Forum 
Date: August 24–26


Location: Victoria, British Columbia


Description: Alexandra and Hamish Khamisa, our President, enjoyed connecting with sustainability leaders and attending the forum’s dynamic sessions, co-hosted by the University of Victoria and the Senate of Canada. Read our full review here.


 


CWMA Annual Conference


Date: October 8–10


Location: Victoria, British Columbia 


Description: Alexandra and Hamish headed back to Victoria for the Coast Waste Management Association’s (CWMA) Annual Conference. As a proud CWMA Heritage Preservation Partner, we shared CWMA’s story leading up to the event and introduced CWMA’s Executive Director at the conference.


 


Recycling Council of Alberta Circular Economy Conference


Date: October 22–24


Location: Jasper, Alberta


Description: Alexandra had a stellar time “Shoot[ing] for the Stars” at this year’s Recycling Council of Alberta’s Circular Economy Conference, focused on advancing the circular economy across industries.


 


Greater Purpose Conference East


Date: October 27–28


Location: Toronto, Ontario


Description: A leading event for purpose-driven professionals in Canada, Alexandra and Hamish were excited to attend this conference, which brought together changemakers from multiple sectors to explore social impact and purpose. 


 


Market Close With Blue Jays Legend


Date: October 31


Location: Toronto, Ontario


Description: This year’s historic World Series run was extra special as Elisabeth Choi, our Senior Designer, joined our friends at the Canadian Securities Exchange and Blue Jays baseball legend, José Bautista, who rang the bell to close the market on October 31, in support of the Bautista Impact Fund, in partnership with SickKids Foundation.
